Welcome

A Legacy of Excellence in Health Education

Ridhi Sidhi College of Health Education is a premier institution in Jharkhand, dedicated to providing quality education in pharmaceutical and nursing sciences. Recognized by PCI (Pharmacy Council of India) with Code 6938, we stand as a beacon of healthcare education.

Our college combines rigorous academic curriculum with practical training, ensuring our graduates are industry-ready from day one. With state-of-the-art laboratories, digital classrooms, and a caring faculty, we nurture the next generation of healthcare professionals.

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To provide exceptional pharmaceutical and nursing education that empowers students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical foundation needed to serve communities across India and the world. We are committed to innovation in teaching and fostering clinical excellence through hands-on training.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred institution for health sciences education in Eastern India, recognized for academic excellence, research innovation, and producing graduates who make a meaningful difference in global healthcare. We envision a world where quality health education is accessible to all.
